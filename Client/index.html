Welcome to POSTCARDMANIA Service

Follow these steps to successfully create and send your campaigns:


  1. Choose Your Starting Point: You have two options on the dashboard: "New Campaign" or "New Mailer". Start by creating either a campaign or designing a mailer.
  2. Select & Save Your Audience: Once your campaign and mailer are ready, choose your recipients using either "Upload CSV" or "Batch Search" to select your audience. Then save your audience to move forward.
  3. Choose or Edit a Template: You can select an existing template or edit it according to your needs. Any template you edit will automatically be available for your campaign. You can edit canva templates and send them.
  4. Create or Send Your Letter: You have two options: "Create and Send" to immediately send the letter, or "Create and Save" to save the campaign or one-off mailer for later use. You can schedule them in dashboard.
  5. Order Tracking and Insights Dashboard: Access all campaign details in one place. The dashboard lets users schedule pending letters, manage multiple mailers per campaign, and track recipient engagement, such as whether a letter has been scanned.

Enjoy using PostcRDMANIA to automate your mailing efficiently!
