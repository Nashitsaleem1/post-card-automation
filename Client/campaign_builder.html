RES OCC Service EDDM

Residential Occupancy mailing reaches every household in selected carrier routes. No recipient list needed!

  • No recipient list required
  • Target by carrier routes and demographics
  • Reaches every household in selected areas
  • Cost-effective for broad coverage

  4. Select Carrier Routes - Choose one or more routes from the dropdown (appears after step 3)
  5. Add Demographics (Optional) - Refine your audience with filters
  6. Get List Count - Review recipient count and place your order

Welcome to POSTCARDMANIA Service

Follow these steps to successfully create and send your campaigns:


  1. Choose Your Campaign Type: Select either "New Campaign" or "New Mailer" to get started.
  2. Select Your Audience (Optional for RES OCC): Upload a CSV, use Batch Search, or select an existing audience. Skip this step if using RES OCC Service.
  3. Choose a Template: Select from our gallery or use Canva to design your letter.
  4. Choose Mailing Method: Select between Direct Mail (for specific recipients) or RES OCC Service (for carrier route targeting).
  5. Send or Save: Send your campaign immediately or save it as a draft for later scheduling.

Enjoy using POSTCARDMANIA to automate your mailing efficiently!
